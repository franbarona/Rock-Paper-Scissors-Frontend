Instructions

Ready to play? Follow these simple steps to get started!

Game Objective

Beat the computer by choosing one of three options: Rock, Paper, or Scissors. The winner is determined according to the following classic rules:

Rules

Rock beats Scissors

Rock crushes scissors

Paper beats Rock

Paper covers rock

Scissors beats Paper

Scissors cut paper

If both players choose the same, it's a draw

How to Play

  1. Click on your choice: Rock, Paper, or Scissors
  2. The computer will automatically choose its option
  3. The result will be determined based on the rules
  4. Your score will be updated automatically
  5. Play as many times as you want!
